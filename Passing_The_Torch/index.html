Passing the torch: A new dawn for Astrae

Author: Vivek Menon

Dear… well, everyone!

Wowwee, this is great!

Thanks a tonne, and we cannot wait

To continue, and do something new, and

Oh, gowsh, there’s so much to do! #(excited squeal)

On this auspicious date,

As the daylight sinks due West,

We shall carry this torch*

And we hope to do our best.

We’ll plan and we’ll strive, though we’ve big shoes to fill,

And maybe we’ll try to climb higher still…

Clear skies!

Animesh, Sarthak, and Vivek

P.S. For the best effect, read it in the tune of the first two stanzas of the song “Passing Through” by Kaden MacKay.

*With a red-light filter on it, of course 😉
